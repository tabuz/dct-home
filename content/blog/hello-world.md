The biggest mistake in construction technology isn't bad software — it's the assumption that legacy systems need to be replaced.

## The Rip-and-Replace Fallacy

Every year, millions are spent on ERP migration projects that stall, overrun, or outright fail. The reason is simple: these systems aren't just software. They're the institutional memory of decades of commercial logic, supplier relationships, and financial workflows.

## A Different Approach

At DCT, we've spent years developing a methodology we call **Headless ERP Architecture**. Instead of competing with your ledger, we build the intelligence layer that wraps around it.

This means:

- **Zero disruption** to your core transactional engine
- **Secure, real-time APIs** that surface data where your teams actually work
- **Modern interfaces** — from Progressive Web Apps to AI-powered voice tools — that connect directly to your existing data

## The Result

Your teams get modern velocity. Your finance department keeps their trusted ledger. And your business avoids the operational heart attack of a full system migration.

That's not a compromise. That's engineering done right.
