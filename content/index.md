::problem{headline="Liberating the Data Prison."}
The UK construction supply chain runs on highly complex, on-premise ERP systems. These platforms are formidable financial ledgers but rigid data prisons for your sales, commercial, and operations teams.

Generic technology agencies will tell you to abandon them. We know better.

You need digital innovation, but you cannot afford the operational disruption of replacing your core transactional engine. You need an engineering partner who understands how to securely extract that data and put it to work — without ever breaking what already works.

items:
  - question: Do you replace our ERP system?
    answer: Never. Our entire model is built around protecting your core
      transactional engine. We build the intelligence and integration layer that
      surrounds your ERP — extracting, linking, and surfacing data through
      modern APIs and applications, without touching the ledger your business
      runs on. That's not a constraint; it's our core competency.
  - question: Which ERP systems do you support?
    answer: We have deep experience with SAP, Kerridge (K8/Autopart), Epicor, and
      custom legacy platforms common in the UK construction supply chain. Our
      middleware architecture is system-agnostic — if it stores
      business-critical data, we have an approach to surface it safely.
  - question: How long does a typical engagement take?
    answer: It depends on scope and complexity. A targeted Microsoft 365 integration
      or email sidecar can go live in 6–8 weeks. A full data infrastructure
      project with field applications and AI orchestration typically spans 3–6
      months. We'll give you a realistic, milestone-based timeline in the
      architecture proposal — not a sales estimate.
  - question: Do we need a large internal IT team?
    answer: No. Many of our clients have lean IT functions. We operate as your
      embedded engineering partner, managing everything from architecture
      through deployment and ongoing support. We work alongside whoever you have
      internally — whether that's one person or a full team.
  - question: How do you handle data security and access control?
    answer: All solutions are built on Google Cloud Platform with role-based access
      controls, encrypted data pipelines, and audit logging as baseline
      non-negotiables. We never require or store raw ERP credentials in external
      systems. Data flows exclusively through secure middleware we architect,
      own, and maintain.
  - question: What kind of business is this the right fit for?
    answer: We work exclusively with large, established UK construction businesses —
      typically £50M+ revenue with complex ERP infrastructure and genuine
      operational pain. Our work isn't a good fit for early-stage companies or
      off-the-shelf software rollouts. If you're operating at enterprise scale
      with systems that demand specialist engineering attention, we want to hear
      from you.
